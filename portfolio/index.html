Non-Profit

riverainc.org (2023 - Present). RIVERA Intiatives Inc. (Resource Initiative for Venturous Education & Research in Academia) is a a Puerto Rico 501(c)(3)-recognized Private Foundation established in San Juan, Puerto Rico that seeks to empower academia through comprehensive resources and collaborative research endeavors to advance educational excellence and innovation.

A branch of RIVERA Intiatives Inc., RIVERA-OMICS (Omics Molecular Innovations and Commercialization Strategies) is an academic research group that focuses on decoding biology through computational methods that integrate multi-level omics data with the goal of designing novel therapeutic interventions and diagnostic strategies. The group is actively recruiting students seeking an opportunity to make outstanding contributions to research propjects and peer-reviewed publications.
