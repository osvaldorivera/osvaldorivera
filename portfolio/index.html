Portfolio





genos.bio (2023 - Present). GENOS LLC is a Puerto Rican business being built to empower biolabs studying disease with bioinformatics capabilities by contributing to the implementation of data processing pipelines as well as pushing scientific projects through custom analyses. The business currently focuses on providing products and services related to genomics research, but is expanding to include molecular dynamics research products.


riveraomics.org (2023 - Present). The OMICS Group is a newly established acadamic lab in San Juan, Puerto Rico, which focuses on decoding biology through computational methods that integrate multi-level omics data with the goal of designing novel therapeutic intervantions and diagnostic strategies. The group is actively recruiting students seeking an opportunity to make outstanding contributions to research propjects and peer-reviewed publications.
